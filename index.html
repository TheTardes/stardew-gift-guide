About Game

Welcome to Stardew Valley Gift Guide!

This website is your personal guide for navigating friendship in the world of Stardew Valley.

Stardew Valley is a farming sim where you inherit your grandfather’s old farm but the real heart of the game is building friendships with the townsfolk.

Each character has unique likes, routines, and personalities. Gifting them their favorites boosts friendship, unlocks cutscenes, recipes, letters even romance.

Whether you're just starting your farm or already deep into year three, giving the right gifts makes building relationships easier and more fun.

How to use

This site helps you explore every NPC’s gift preferences in Stardew Valley

  • 1.Use the dropdown menu or Character Cards to select any NPC.
  • 2.View their gift categories: Loved, Liked, Neutral, Disliked, Hated.
  • 3.Explore Universal Gifts like “Universal Likes” and “Universal Hates.”
  • 4.Click the category tags to jump between sections quickly.
  • 5.Click NPC List to go back and choose another character.

Works smoothly on both desktop and mobile. Designed for fast reference while playing!
